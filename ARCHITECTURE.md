## Domain Model (from SPEC-COST-001 §5)

14 Prisma models in 4 concern groups:

**Master data**: Organization (3-level hierarchy), Project, Personnel, CostCategory, StandardRate, TransferMarkup

**Transactional**: CostEntry (grain: personnel × project × date × period), Period (OPEN/CLOSED state machine)

**Allocation engine**: AllocationRule, AllocationRun (audit), AllocationResult

**Analytics**: TransferEntry, VarianceSnapshot, AuditLog

All monetary fields: `Decimal @db.Decimal(18, 4)` → PostgreSQL `DECIMAL(18,4)`.
